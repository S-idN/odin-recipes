Starbucks Caramel Frappuccino (at home!)

Description

This is Allrecipes' version of Starbucks Caramel Frappuccino! It's easy to make at home by blending coffee with milk, caramel sauce, and ice.
Top each glass with whipped cream and drizzle more caramel sauce on top. Serve with a straw.

Ingredients Required~~

  • 2 cups ice
  • 1 cup strongly brewed black coffee, cooled
  • 1 cup low-fat milk
  • 1/3 caramel sauce
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar

Instructions~~

  1. Step 1

    Blend ice, coffee, milk, caramel sauce, and sugar together in a blender on high speed until smooth.

  2. Step 2

    Pour into two 16-ounce glasses.
